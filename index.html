about bobigos

bobigos is a free network that connects small and first-time founders to build ventures that stand out. we believe in the power of collaboration and helping small businesses grow through meaningful partnerships and going digital, allowing our 600+ users to grow 24/7 and bring PTO vibes to entrepreneurship.

"bobigos" combines "boba" with "amigos" (spanish for friends). it's about building friendships and partnerships while enhancing or starting your business/startup â€” like enjoying boba tea with friends while brainstorming!

we offer a library full of potential partners and niche startup ideas that have worked for our users, and a supportive community where small founders can grow their ventures while staying true to their unique creative vision. we also post startup/business ideas daily on our instagram (@bobigos), as well as featuring users who join the community for free on our website and on our social media pages.

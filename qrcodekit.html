Your Quick Guide to Launching Your Startup Online

What It Means to Build a Startup or Business Today

Starting a business is about solving problems and sharing your passion.

  • In today’s world, digital tools make it easier than ever to reach customers and build a community — even if you’re just starting out!
  • Whether you’re selling a product, offering a service, or sharing your story, having a strong digital presence helps you grow.

Why Digitalization Matters & How CTRL Helps

Digitalization means using the internet and social media to connect with your audience — this can be:

  • Creating a social media page (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
  • Curating your feed to tell your brand story visually
  • Building an online community around your business

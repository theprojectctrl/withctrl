3-Step Guide for Crafting a Niche / Signature

Click each step to unfold the process for creating your unique niche tagline

Step 1: Identify Your Hyperspecific Area

Focus on a narrow, well-defined part of your field.

Ask yourself: Who am I serving? What problem am I solving? What specific skill or topic am I focusing on?

Example prompts:

  • "Rural students learning to code"
  • "Teen literary zines"
  • "Sustainable skincare products"

Step 2: Choose Your Signature Element

Pick a defining object, tool, color, philosophy, or method that gives your venture a unique flavor.

This adds: Recognition, personality, and consistency.
